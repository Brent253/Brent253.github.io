About Me

Born and Raised in Tacoma Washington.


I am currently a senior undergraduate at Green River College.
I grew up with a strong interest of becoming an artist due to a constant exposure of playing video games as a child.
I always had a passion for computers, I was hooked on playing runescape for a large portion of my child hood and would slowly pursue other games such as Guild wars 2, League of legends, and Hearthstone.
I chose to pursue software development because I wanted a career where I had the potential to create something for an audience that could represent my sense of style.
Though I wasn't completely in love with the idea about getting into the software industry I found that I had a visual mindset for designing websites and creating projects on topics I could relate to.
